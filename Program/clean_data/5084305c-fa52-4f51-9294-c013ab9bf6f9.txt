Witnesses on the ground “filmed as a plane weaved its way its way through the intimidating tornado-like spouts in preparation to land” in Sochi, Russia on Tuesday.

Witnesses in Sochi filmed as the plane weaved its way through water spouts which formed over the Black Sea The incident happened Tuesday when 12 huge water spouts appeared just off the southwestern Russian coast At least nine flights were diverted as a result of the menacing weather in Russia and had to land elsewhere

Apparently “up to twelve tornadoes were spotted off the coast on the same day” amidst two weeks of significant weather events on the Black Sea creating challenging flying conditions.
