ATM S 101 Weather (5) NWThe earth's atmosphere, with emphasis on weather observations and forecasting. Daily weather map discussions. Highs, lows, fronts, clouds, storms, jet streams, air pollution, and other features of the atmosphere. Physical processes involved in weather phenomena. Intended for nonmajors. Offered: AWSpS.

UW students and outside folks can take this class. If you are over 60, low cost registration is possible with the UW Access program ( https://registrar.washington.edu/course-registration/access-program/
