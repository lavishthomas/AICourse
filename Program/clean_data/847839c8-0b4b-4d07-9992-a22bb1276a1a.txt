Naruko is a cult Taiwanese line of anti-aging skincare products. Their line of night gellies features an incredibly light texture and exotic ingredients like alpine skullcap flower extract and alpine willowherb.

The most intriguing elixir is the Classic Hydrating Night Gelly, which features rose and snow fungus, a fungus formally known as Tremella fuciformis which works as an antioxidant to seal in moisture and replenish aging skin. Available here.
